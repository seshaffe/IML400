Research

I've been exploring lots of searchable online databases in order to draw inspiration for my project. I've tested out everything from USC's library catalogue to an obituary search to just Google itself! I have concluded that my site will benefit most from a search feature that requires a "search by" selection, and then allows the search to be more open or restricted based on the selected category.
