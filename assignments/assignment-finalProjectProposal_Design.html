My website will have three main branches: a browse branch, a search branch, and a branch for special lists. Selecting browse will lead to a page displaying all my DVDs and information about them. Selecting search will allow users to access a customizable search feature. Selecting special lists will allow users to enjoy a library of lists I have created.
